Riyadh region location.

The Riyadh region is located in the center of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

The area of ​​the Riyadh region is approximately 380,000 km2.

Climate of the region

The Riyadh region currently has a desert climate.

Suggested places to visit

Saudi National Museum

From the Earth to the Moon, and from prehistoric times to the modern era, the corridors of the Saudi National Museum take you on an exciting journey, transcending time and space, and standing on artistic and historical evidence in the heart of the city of Riyadh..

Edge of the world

An ideal destination for mountain climbing and hiking enthusiasts, at the end of the Tuwaiq Mountains, which extend about 800 km, there is a high overlook called the Edge of the World, at an altitude of 1,131 m and 100 km from the city of Riyadh. Step out to discover a breathtaking, enchanting view.

Discover the Riyadh Zoo

While traveling to the capital, Riyadh, whether you are with your children or friends, do not miss the opportunity to visit the zoo, which includes more than 1,300 animals from 190 species in 6 natural protected areas. Its visit will fill you with joy and entertainment.
