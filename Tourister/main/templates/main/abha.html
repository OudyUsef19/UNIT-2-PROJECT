Abha region location.

The city of Abha has an ideal location in southwestern Saudi Arabia, at an altitude of 2,200 meters between the peaks of the Sarawat Mountains.

Area of ​​the Abha region

Abha extends over an area of ​​5,100 hectares, with the area of ​​the city of Abha being 201,912 square kilometers.

Tourist destination

n 2017, Abha was awarded the title of Arab Tourism Capital Award by the Arab Tourism Organization in the Arab countries.

Suggested places to visit

Art Street

Enjoy the events held on Art Street in the summer, coinciding with the blooming of the jacaranda trees surrounding it, in their breathtaking purple color. Do not let confusion delay you from choosing between attending art exhibitions by local artists.
